OUR INTRODUCTION

Top Medical Billing Company in India

Up to 40%
Savings in Cost

vitalink India is one of the top medical billing companies in India that provides complete medical billing solutions and coding services. vitalink is a pioneer in offshore medical billing, having started in the year 2000 - one of the first few to offer medical billing services from India at that time. Today, vitalink has grown to 1,500+ people and 3 delivery centers that provide Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity provisions. vitalink possesses twin ISO certifications - ISO 9001:2015 for our operational processes; and ISO 27001:2013 for ISMS (Information Security and Management systems) that together assure some of the highest security levels in the way PHI is handled, making vitalink compliant with the latest HIPAA rules.

vitalink is unique amongst offshore medical billing and medical coding companies in India, in that it works with clients of all sizes - no client is too small or too large and they all get the same level of attention. While we have the scale and resources of a large offshore medical billing company in India, we also ensure that we have the agility and flexibility of a small company to provide dedicated and focused medical billing companies for all our clients.

vitalink provides medical billing services that comprise the following functions:

  • Patient Registration/Demographic Entry
  • Denial Management
  • Medical Coding Services
  • Insurance Eligibility Verification
  • Charge entry Services
  • Physician/Facility Credentialing
  • EDI-Set up/Electronic Claims transmission
  • Indexing Medical Records
  • Payment Posting
  • Data Conversion
  • Accounts Receivable follow up
  • Patient AR / Balance

vitalink serves Healthcare Revenue Cycle companies and through them, Physicians in the United States by helping them reduce their operational and administrative costs, without compromising on revenue flows. Backed by extensive domain expertise, latest technology and robust compliance norms, vitalink offers 24x7 offshore medical billing services enabling our long list of clients in the United States to focus and transform into high performance businesses.
